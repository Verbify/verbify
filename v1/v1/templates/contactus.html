how can we help you?

verbify is a community, and as such there are a lot of outlets to get help for what ails you.

  1. get help moderating

    • Are you a new moderator? Need advice? You'll find a community ready to assist you at /r/modhelp.

  2. report a bug

    • Check out /r/bugs for other people with the same problem, or submit your own bug report.
    • If you have an idea for a new feature, tell us about it in /r/ideasfortheadmins.

  3. use the verbify trademark

    • You'll need a license to use the verbify trademark. Read our licensing page to find out how to get permission.

  4. make a press enquiry

    • You can email us at press@verbify.com or call us at +1 424-234-9948.

  5. advertise on verbify

    • Subscribe to /r/selfserve to talk with other advertisers about advertising on verbify.
    • Check out /r/ads to see the most popular image ads on verbify.
    • Reach the verbify advertising team at advertising@verbify.com.
    • Learn more about advertising products and best practices at verbify.com/advertising.

  6. ask a general question

    • Maybe you want to /r/askverbify? Or for help try making a post at /r/help.
    • Need help with a verbifygifts exchange? Email support@verbifygifts.com.
    • Got a question about verbify sodium? Please email ${g.sodiumsupport_email}.
    • Anything we didn't cover? Email us at contact@verbify.com and include your verbify username if you have one.

  7. message the admins

    • Need to contact the admins? You can message them here.
    • Need to file a DMCA takedown request? Please email dmca@verbify.com with a link to the content on verbify and all pertinent information.
